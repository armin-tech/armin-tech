Problem

Identifying root cause to failure is a great challenge in most electromechanical industries.

18%-30% of maintenance is wasted due to lack of good and relevant maintenance practice. In addition upto 30% of maintenance is assumed damaging for the equipment.

Our Philosophy

We believe that the people working closely with the equipment everyday are essential for a good maintenance practice. The input from these workers, their feedback from inspecting, monitoring, troubleshooting and repairing are vital. Analysis are not better than the data put into it.

This feedback combined with the world class maintenance practice is essential to achieve optimum availability and minimum sustainable cost of operation and maintenance.

Our Solution

Armin-tech combines all input from practical maintenance work, expert evaluation and sensors together with advanced machine learning to achieve optimal maintenance.

Faultfinding and optimizing maintenance is made easy and accurate combining all relevant input from maintenance organisation, expert evaluations and sensors together with machine learning.

Benefits

Effective troubleshooting with targeting potensial root causes.

Directly linking feedback from maintenance personnel to optimizing maintenance.

Effective training and transfer of experiences.

Effective predicting fault development and residual lifespan.

Increased commitment and enthusism among workers.

Effective tool for sparepart analysis.

Effective benchmarking of equipment across the company.
